== Description ==

The official Notice Wordpress Plugin. Notice allows you to create, translate, and deploy content quickly and easily everywhere on the web.

**Jumpstart Your Content Creation with Diverse Templates:** Notice is equipped with an array of templates suitable for various needs, including FAQs, blog posts, terms of use, privacy policies, job boards, and investor reports. These templates provide a solid foundation, preventing the common issue of facing a blank page.

**Generate Content with Ease:** With Notice, you can effortlessly generate content for any topic, ensuring a constant flow of ideas and maintaining a steady stream of engaging content.

**Why Notice is an Indispensable Tool:**

- **Centralized Content Management:** Notice serves as a central hub for all your content-related tasks. It allows you to create, edit, update, and translate from a single, user-friendly interface.
- **Efficiency and Productivity:** Streamline your content creation process, saving valuable time and resources.
- **Consistent Synchronization:** Ensure your content remains consistent and updated across all platforms.

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* WordPress 5.0 or greater
* PHP version 6.0 or greater

= Recommended Requirements =

* WordPress 6.0 or greater
* PHP version 7.4 or greater

= Installation =

1. Install using the WordPress built-in Plugin installer, or Extract the zip file and drop the contents in the `wp-content/plugins/` directory of your WordPress installation.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Optional: Sign In/Up inside the embedded Notice editor
4. Create your project (FAQ, Blog, Article, etc.) and publish it
5. Go to Integrations → WordPress → Copy your ID project
6. Come back to WordPress → add a new block → select Notice
7. Drag and drop the Notice block anywhere you want on your page and paste the ID project
8. Click on Update, and than View page to check your project live

For documentation and tutorials visit our [Wordpress Integration](https://integrations.notice.site/wordpress-g7dqh4j15j).

== Screenshots ==

1. Find Notice Dashboard on the left side menu after installing and activating the Notice plugin

2. Connect to your Notice account to start creating your content

3. Choose between ready-to-use templates, type of projects, or start from scratch

4. Intuitive no-code editor - type ‘/’ to find all block possibilities such as paragraphs, bullet lists, images, expandable for FAQs, and much more

5. Create together with our AI assistant that can generate text and images to make your creating flow easier and fast

6. When you’re finished creating, go to Integrations and copy your Project ID - if you use Elementor, copy the second one

7. Add a new block, search for Notice (or Shortcode if you use Elementor), drag and drop the notice block anywhere you want on your page, and paste the ID project inside

8. Click on Update, then View Page, and check that your project is live into your WordPress website

9. Extra features: translate your content in 100+ languages and offer a better experience for your audience

10. Extra feature: gain insights with our Analytics - check visits, location and device info of your users, what they’re looking for on the search bar, and much more
